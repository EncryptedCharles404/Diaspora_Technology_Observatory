The Process

From Patent Database to Your Factory Floor

We do the research so your engineers and procurement teams don't have to.

01
We Find It
We search WIPO PATENTSCOPE, USPTO, Espacenet, and Google Patents for technologies in your sector whose patents have expired — verified for your jurisdiction.
02
We Verify It
We confirm expiry dates, confirm jurisdiction coverage across African patent systems (ARIPO, OAPI, national offices), and identify WIPO programmes and Medicines Patent Pool access routes.
03
We Map It
We produce a sector-specific Technology Access Report with saving potential, regulatory pathway, WIPO programme links, and implementation notes specific to your country.
04
You Save
Your company implements technology at zero licensing cost. We document the outcome — building Africa's public domain manufacturing knowledge base.
