# Diaspora Technology Observatory
### Nigeria Patent Intelligence Portal

> *Helping Nigerian manufacturers stop overpaying for technology that is already free.*

---

## What This Is

The **Diaspora Technology Observatory (DTO)** is a research and advisory platform that maps industrial technologies available to Nigerian businesses at **zero licensing cost** — through expired patents, WIPO programmes, and international development frameworks most Nigerian companies don't know exist.

This repository hosts the DTO Patent Intelligence Portal — a live, searchable database of verified public domain patents and free technology access routes for Nigerian manufacturers.

## What the Portal Does

- 🔍 **Searchable patent library** — filter by sector, access type, and keyword
- 📊 **12+ verified entries** across Pharmaceuticals, Energy, Steel, Agriculture, Digital Infrastructure, Automotive, and Cement
- 📋 **Implementation notes** for each patent — NAFDAC pathways, WIPO programmes, legal frameworks
- 📩 **Report request form** — visitors submit their sector and receive a tailored Technology Access Report within 48 hours
- 🔐 **Admin panel** — password-protected database management for the DTO team

## The Opportunity

Nigerian manufacturers spend **billions annually** licensing technology that has been in the public domain for decades. When a patent expires after 20 years, any company anywhere in the world can use that technology at zero cost.

Most Nigerian businesses do not know this exists.

The DTO finds it, maps it, and shows manufacturers exactly how to use it — legally, safely, and for free.
